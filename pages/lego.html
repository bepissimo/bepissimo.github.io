What's this?

The lego box is an idea I've been toying with for the better part of a year now.
When I was in elementary school, I had unbridled creativity and felt no creative limitations or insecurities, academic or otherwise.
However, as I progressed through school, I lost a lot of that creativity and my passion to just create for the sake of creation.
If I created something, it had to be for a purpose, whether it be to improve my comprehension of a topic or to satisfy a requirement.
I was also comparing my work to others' around me. This made me incredibly insecure, and almost made me scared to be creative at all.
I always felt like no matter how hard I tried, nothing I made could stand up to the works of my peers.
That's why this page exists.
One of my fondest memories growing up was having a large red lego box full of disassembled kits.
I would then use these pieces to build new things, completely out of my imagination.
Although they never looked as good as what came from the original kit, they were my own creations.
I never expected them to accomplish anything or be of noteworthy quality. I just wanted to have fun.
That's what lies ahead on the button below.
By pressing the button below, you will probably find nothing of quality.
It won't be impressive. Compared to what others are making, it will be arguably worthless.
If you're looking for impressive creations, don't waste your time.
Go back to LinkedIn. I'm sure people have plenty of things to brag about over there.
All I can assure you is that what is created is mine.
Everything below is created purely out of passion.
